About Us

Be Cool About Wildfiresis a nonprofit focused on helping communities affected by wildfires. We connect victims with volunteers and resources to provide immediate relief. In addition to emergency support, we raise awareness and collaborate with local authorities to improve wildfire preparedness. ...Our mission is to build stronger communities and provide ongoing support to those in need.

Our Mission

Our mission is to offer immediate assistance and relief to those impacted by wildfires by connecting them with willing volunteers. We beleive in the strength of community support to provide hope and essential resources to those in need.

Request Assistance

If you’re affected by a wildfire, Be Cool About Wildfires can help you find the support you need. We connect survivors with local volunteers who provide shelter, food, water, and other essential resources. Fill out our simple form, and we’ll match you with the right support, including emergency supplies and counseling services, to assist you through this difficult time. ...After submitting the form, our team will review the request and reach out to you as soon as possible. Please ensure that all required fields are filled out carefully. Thanks you for reaching out to us for assistance.
